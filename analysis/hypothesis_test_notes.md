# Hypothesis Test Notes
Null Hypothesis (H0): Treatment conversion rate equals Control conversion rate.
Alternative Hypothesis (H1): Treatment conversion rate is higher than Control conversion rate.
Test: Two-sample t-test.
Alpha: 0.05
Primary Metric: Paid Conversion Rate.
Control Conversion: 0.0317
Treatment Conversion: 0.0699
P-value: 0.001066
Decision: Reject H0 because p-value < 0.05.
Business Interpretation: Treatment significantly improves paid conversion.
